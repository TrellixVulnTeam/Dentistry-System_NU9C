Restorative Dentistry Clinical Handbook 2018

This book has been created as an introduction to the clinics at Leeds Dental Institute and it contains information about the clinics (equipment, responsibilities expected from the student/staff such as professional conduct, patient management...) and general clinical working practices (infection prevention and control, x-ray facilities...) as well as aspects related to the different restorative clinical procedures carried out in clinics.
